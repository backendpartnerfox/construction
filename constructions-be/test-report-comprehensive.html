Complete Business Workflow

1

Enquiry Stage

Customer inquiry received via marketing campaigns. Basic requirements captured, package selection made, and initial quotation generated.

Routes: enquiries, enquiry_requirements, enquiry_quotations, marketing_campaigns
2

Lead Management

Qualified enquiries converted to leads. Detailed requirements gathered, multiple quotation versions managed, site visits scheduled.

Routes: leads, lead_requirements, lead_quotations, lead_quotation_history
3

Client Conversion

Lead accepts quotation and makes initial payment. Client created, project initiated, and team assigned.

Routes: clients, finance_payments, projects, client_requirements, client_quotations
4

Design & Measurement

Architect conducts site visit, takes measurements, creates drawings, and gets client approval.

Routes: architect_measurements_*, architect_drawings, site_visits
5

BOQ Generation

Based on measurements and element mappings, quantities are calculated and BOQ is generated for all categories.

Routes: project_boq_*, items_*_calculations, element_item_mapping
6

Component Breakdown

Requirements divided into components (structural, electrical, plumbing) and further into units (floor-wise breakdown).

Routes: components, component_units, project_components, units
7

Costing & Vendor Selection

Material costing calculated, vendor pricing compared, and optimal vendors selected for each item.

Routes: project_material_costing, vendor_pricing, sourcing_item_tmt_bar_pricing
8

Project Planning

Units grouped into phases, scheduling created, client selections managed, and work sequencing defined.

Routes: phases, phase_units, selections, blocks, sequencing
9

Procurement & Execution

Purchase orders created, work orders issued, materials procured, and work packages executed.

Routes: purchase_orders, work_orders, modules, work_packages, execution_tracking
